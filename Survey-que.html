Survey and Questionnaires

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

By asking these questions, we can gather information about potential data leakage and take appropriate measures to prevent it. It is important to collect and store this data in a secure and responsible manner, and to ensure that it is used only for the purposes for which it was collected. It is also important to provide users with clear and concise information about how their data is collected, used, and shared, and to obtain their consent where required by law.

Questionnaire

1. Have you shared your personal information with any third-party vendors or partners?



2. Have you noticed any unusual activity on your account or in your communication with the website? This question can help you detect any potential data leakage through unauthorized access or hacking.



3. Have you received any suspicious emails or messages that appear to be from the website? This question can help you detect any potential data leakage through phishing or social engineering attacks.



4. Have you clicked on any suspicious links or downloaded any suspicious files while using the website? This question can help you detect any potential data leakage through malware or other malicious software.



5. Have you shared your login credentials with anyone else?



6. Have you used any public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks while using the website?



7. Have you noticed any changes in your transaction history or payment information?



8. Have you shared your login credentials with anyone else?

Community

Data leakage detection is an important aspect of data security and privacy. A community on data leakage detection can help organizations and individuals share information, best practices, and resources for detecting and preventing data leakage.
